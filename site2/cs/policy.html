Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Introduction to Privacy Policy

+

Qezvoro Investment Solutions Ltd. ("we", "company", "Qezvoro Invest") attaches fundamental importance to the protection of your personal data and ensuring privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the principles of collecting, processing, protecting, and transferring information about you when interacting with our services, including the web platform, mobile applications, and related tools (collectively, the "Qezvoro platform").

By using the Qezvoro trading platform services, you agree to the processing of information in accordance with the principles set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Categories of Information Collected

+

Personal Identification Information

  • Basic Information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact Details: Postal address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial Profile: Information on income, sources of capital, trading experience, and investment goals
  • Verification Documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, proof of income, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photographic image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures

Qezvoro Platform Usage Data

  • Transaction History: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account status
  • Technical Parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral Analysis: Website navigation, session duration, user preferences, search queries

Information from External Sources

  • Partner Details: Information from brokerage firms, partner banks, market analysis providers
  • Open Sources: Data from public registers, media sources, social platforms (publicly available information only)
  • Advisory Information: Information obtained from checking recommendations and references

Purposes of Personal Data Processing

+

Provision of Investment Services

  • Creation and management of user accounts
  • Execution of trading orders and investment operations
  • Generate personalized AI recommendations and analytical reports
  • Processing deposit operations, fund top-ups, and withdrawals
  • Providing technical support and customer service

Compliance with Regulations

  • Adherence to KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures
  • Reporting to financial regulators
  • Detection and prevention of fraudulent activities
  • Compliance with tax obligations and FATCA/CRS requirements

Enhancement of the Qezvoro Invest Platform

  • Analysis of user behavior to improve UI/UX
  • Development of innovative products and functionalities
  • Optimization of AI algorithms (using depersonalized data)
  • Conducting market research and trend analysis

Communication Objectives

  • Notifications about trading operation status and account changes
  • Information about new products, Qezvoro platform updates
  • Provision of educational content and market insights
  • Conducting service quality surveys

Principles of Data Transfer to Third Parties

+

Technology Solution Providers

  • Cloud Providers: Microsoft Azure, AWS, Google Cloud for hosting and data processing
  • Payment Systems: Acquiring banks, payment processors for financial transactions
  • Information Services: Refinitiv, S&P Global for market data
  • KYC Services: Jumio, Sumsub for document verification and identification

Regulatory and Supervisory Authorities

  • Financial supervisory authorities (SEC, FCA, CySEC, FINMA, etc.) within mandatory reporting
  • Tax administrations to comply with FATCA, CRS, and national tax laws
  • Law enforcement agencies, in the presence of legitimate official requests

Business Partners

  • Exchanges and brokerage firms for executing trading orders
  • Custodian banks for safekeeping client assets
  • Insurance companies providing insurance coverage

Important: We absolutely DO NOT SELL or rent your personal information to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Data Protection and Security Measures

+

Technical Protection Measures:

  • Cryptographic Protection: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Control: Multi-factor authentication, role-based authorization system
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring for threats and unauthorized access attempts
  • Backups: Regular backups in geographically distributed data centers

Organizational Measures:

  • Employee Training: Systematic information security training
  • Company Policies: Strict internal regulations for personal data processing
  • Independent Audits: Annual security audits by Deloitte and other audit firms
  • Data Segregation: Physical and logical separation of client and company information

Data Retention Periods and Cookies

+

General Principles:

Personal data is retained only for the period necessary to achieve the purposes of processing or in accordance with legal requirements.

Specific Retention Periods:

  • Details of Active Accounts: Duration of the contract + 8 years after termination
  • KYC Documentation: 8 years after account closure (AML legislation requirements)
  • Transaction Records: 8 years (financial regulator requirements)
  • Marketing Data: Until consent withdrawal or 5 years from last activity
  • Call Records: 7 years (MiFID II requirements)
  • Minor Details: Immediate deletion upon discovery

Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies

Types of Cookies Used:

  • Essential Cookies: Provide basic website functionality (authorization, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Store user preferences and settings
  • Analytical Cookies: Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics for resource usage analysis
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalize advertising content and track campaign effectiveness

Manage Cookie Settings:

You can control the use of cookies through:

  • Web browser settings
  • The cookie control panel on the Qezvoro website
  • Opting out of analytical cookies via the browser add-on for Google Analytics opt-out
