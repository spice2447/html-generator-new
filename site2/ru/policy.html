Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 20, 2025

Introduction to Privacy Policy

+

Qezvoro Investment Solutions Ltd. ("we", "company", "Qezvoro Invest") considers the protection of your personal data and the assurance of privacy to be of critical importance. This Privacy Policy outlines the principles of collecting, processing, protecting, and transferring information about you when interacting with our services, including the web platform, mobile applications, and related tools (collectively – the "Qezvoro platform").

By using the Qezvoro trading platform services, you consent to the processing of information in accordance with the principles set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Categories of information collected

+

Personal identification information

  • Basic information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact details: Postal address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial profile: Information about income, sources of capital, trading experience, and investment goals
  • Verification documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, income statements, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photographic image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures

Information about using the Qezvoro platform

  • Transaction history: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account balances
  • Technical parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral analytics: Website navigation, session duration, user preferences, search queries

Information from external sources

  • Partner data: Information from brokerage companies, partner banks, market analytics providers
  • Public sources: Data from public registries, media sources, social platforms (exclusively publicly available information)
  • Recommendation information: Data obtained during the verification of recommendations and references

Purpose of personal data processing

+

Provision of investment services:

  • Creation and administration of user accounts
  • Execution of trading orders and investment operations
  • Generation of personalized AI recommendations and analytical reports
  • Carrying out depositary operations, deposits, and withdrawals
  • Provision of technical support and client service

Compliance with regulatory requirements:

  • Execution of KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures
  • Submission of reports to financial regulators
  • Detection and prevention of fraudulent activities
  • Fulfillment of tax obligations and FATCA/CRS requirements

Improvement of the QezvoroInvest platform:

  • Analysis of user behavior to improve UX/UI
  • Development of innovative products and functionalities
  • Optimization of AI algorithms (using depersonalized data)
  • Conducting marketing research and trend analysis

Communication purposes:

  • Notifications regarding trading operation status and account changes
  • Information about new products and Qezvoro platform updates
  • Provision of educational content and market analytics
  • Conducting service quality surveys

Principles of data transfer to third parties

+

Technology solution providers:

  • Cloud providers: Microsoft Azure, AWS, Google Cloud for hosting and data processing
  • Payment systems: Acquiring banks, payment processors for financial transactions
  • Information services: Refinitiv, S&P Global for market data acquisition
  • KYC services: Jumio, Sumsub for document verification and identification

Regulatory and supervisory authorities:

  • Financial supervisory bodies (SEC, FCA, CySEC, FINMA, etc.) as part of mandatory reporting
  • Tax authorities to comply with FATCA, CRS, and national tax legislation
  • Law enforcement agencies upon justified official requests

Business partners:

  • Exchanges and brokerage firms for executing trading orders
  • Custodian banks for safekeeping of client assets
  • Insurance organizations for providing insurance coverage

Important: We categorically DO NOT sell or rent your personal data to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Data protection and security measures

+

Technical security measures:

  • Cryptographic protection: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access control: multi-factor authentication, role-based permissions system
  • Security monitoring: round-the-clock threat tracking and unauthorized access attempts
  • Redundancy: regular backups in geographically distributed data centers

Organizational measures:

  • Employee training: systematic information security training
  • Corporate policies: strict internal regulations for personal data processing
  • Independent audits: annual security checks by Deloitte and other audit firms
  • Data segregation: physical and logical separation of client and corporate information

Rights of data subjects:

In accordance with GDPR, you are guaranteed the following rights: the right of access, rectification, erasure ("right to be forgotten"), restriction of processing, data portability, objection to processing based on legitimate interests, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@qezvoro.com.

Storage periods and Cookies

+

Storage periods:

Personal data is stored only for the period necessary to achieve the purposes of processing or in accordance with legal requirements. Specific storage periods include: active account data for the duration of the agreement plus 8 years after termination; KYC documentation for 8 years after account closure; transactional records for 8 years; marketing data until consent withdrawal or 5 years from the last activity; call recordings for 7 years. Data of minors is deleted immediately upon discovery.

Use of Cookies:

We use necessary, functional, analytical, and marketing cookies. You can control cookie settings in your browser or via the Qezvoro website's cookie management panel.
