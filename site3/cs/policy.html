Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction to the Privacy Policy

+

Qezvoro Global Technologies Ltd. ("we", "company", "Qezvoro Invest") attaches the highest importance to protecting your personal data and ensuring privacy. This Privacy Policy explains the principles of collecting, processing, protecting and transferring information about you when interacting with our services, including the web platform, mobile applications and related tools (collectively "Qezvoro platform").

By using Qezvoro services and the trading platform, you agree to the processing of information in accordance with the principles set out in this Privacy Policy.

Categories of Information Collected

+

Personal Identification Data

  • Basic information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact details: Mailing address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial profile: Information about income, capital sources, trading experience and investment goals
  • Verification documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, proof of income, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photographic image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures.

Data on the Use of the Qezvoro Platform

  • Transaction history: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account status
  • Technical parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral analysis: Website navigation, session duration, user preferences, search queries

Information from External Sources

  • Partner details: Information from brokerage companies, partner banks, market analysis providers
  • Open sources: Data from public registers, media sources, social platforms (only publicly available information)
  • Advisory information: Information obtained from checking recommendations and references

Purposes of Personal Data Processing

+

Providing Investment Services:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Executing trading orders and investment operations
  • Generating personalized AI recommendations and analytical reports
  • Performing deposit operations, replenishing and withdrawing funds
  • Providing technical support and customer service

Compliance with Regulations:

  • Compliance with KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures.
  • Reporting to financial regulators
  • Detection and prevention of fraudulent activities
  • Compliance with tax obligations and FATCA/CRS requirements

Improving the Qezvoro Invest Platform:

  • Analyzing user behavior to improve the user interface/user experience
  • Developing innovative products and functionalities
  • Optimizing AI algorithms (using depersonalized data)
  • Conducting marketing research and trend analysis

Communication Goals:

  • Notifications about the status of trading operations and changes to the account
  • Informing about new products, updates to the Qezvoro platform
  • Providing educational content and market information
  • Conducting service quality surveys

Legal Basis for Data Processing

+

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), your data is processed on the following legal bases:

  • Fulfillment of contractual obligations: Processing necessary for fulfilling the investment services contract
  • Legitimate interests of the company: Combating fraud, improving services, marketing communication with existing clients
  • Voluntary consent: Email newsletters, use of analytical cookies, other personalized services
  • Compliance with legal requirements: AML/KYC procedures, tax reporting, regulatory compliance

Principles of Data Transfer to Third Parties

+

Technology Solution Providers:

  • Cloud providers: Google Cloud Platform, AWS, Microsoft Azure for hosting and data processing
  • Payment systems: Acquiring banks, payment processors for financial transactions
  • Information services: Bloomberg Terminal, Reuters for market data
  • KYC services: Onfido, Veriff for verification and document identification

Regulatory and Supervisory Authorities:

  • Financial supervisory authorities (SEC, FCA, BaFin, FINMA, etc.) as part of mandatory reporting
  • Tax authorities to comply with FATCA, CRS and national tax laws
  • Law enforcement agencies, in the presence of valid official requests

Business Partners:

  • Exchanges and brokerage firms for executing trading orders
  • Depository banks for storing client assets
  • Insurance companies providing insurance coverage

Important: We absolutely DO NOT sell or lease your personal data to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Cross-border Data Transfer

+

Your personal data may be processed in jurisdictions outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including the United States, Switzerland, Singapore and other countries where our technology partners operate.

Protective mechanisms for international transfers:

  • Decisions of the European Commission on adequacy for countries that have an adequate level of protection
  • EU standard contractual clauses with partners in third countries
  • Binding corporate rules for intra-company data transfers
  • Special protection for transfers to the United States and other jurisdictions
