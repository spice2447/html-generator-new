History of Qezvoro Development

2020 - Concept Creation

The story began in a Seattle technology hub, where a team of former Google and Goldman Sachs specialists combined their expertise in machine learning and quantum finance. The initial version of the algorithm demonstrated an 81% predictive accuracy, exceeding the results of leading institutional analysts.

2021 - First Results

Launch of the closed beta testing phase with a group of 850 participants from financial hubs in Asia and Europe. During the first year, Qezvoro's trading algorithms provided the test group with an average return of 44%. Receipt of initial investments of $12 million from Sequoia Capital. Obtaining SEC, FCA and BaFin licenses.

2022 - Dynamic Development

Public launch of the platform with exponential growth, increasing from 850 to 28,000 users in 9 months. Opening of the headquarters in Singapore. Strategic alliances with major exchanges: Coinbase, Binance, NASDAQ. Artificial intelligence accuracy reached 91%. Series A from Benchmark Capital: $42 million investment.

2023 - International Expansion

Global presence in over 85 jurisdictions with regional offices in the Americas and Europe. Launch of a mobile app with 1.5 million downloads. Prestigious TechCrunch award "AI Innovation of the Year". User base grew to 47,000, total assets under management of $3.4 billion.

2024 - Industry Leadership

Currently, Qezvoro Invest is among the top 5 most promising fintech projects on the planet with a base of over 55,000 active users and over $5.8 billion in assets under management. The artificial intelligence system has achieved 94% accuracy with continuous self-learning. Preparing for an IPO on NASDAQ in 2025.
