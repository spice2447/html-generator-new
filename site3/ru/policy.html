Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction to Privacy Policy

+

Qezvoro Global Technologies Ltd. ("we", "company", "Qezvoro Invest") attaches primary importance to protecting your personal data and ensuring confidentiality. This Privacy Policy explains the principles of collecting, processing, protecting, and transferring information about you when interacting with our services, including the web platform, mobile applications, and related tools (collectively - "Qezvoro platform").

By using the services of the Qezvoro trading platform, you agree to the processing of information in accordance with the principles set out in this Privacy Policy.

Categories of Information Collected

+

Personal Identification Data

  • Basic information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact information: Mailing address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial profile: Information about income, capital sources, trading experience, and investment goals
  • Verification documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, income statements, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photo image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures

Data on Qezvoro platform usage

  • Transaction history: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account balances
  • Technical parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral analytics: Website navigation, session times, user preferences, search queries

Information from external sources

  • Partner data: Information from brokerage companies, partner banks, market analytics providers
  • Open sources: Data from public registers, media sources, social platforms (exclusively publicly available information)
  • Referral information: Information obtained when verifying recommendations and references

Purposes of Personal Data Processing

+

Providing investment services:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Executing trade orders and investment operations
  • Generating personalized AI recommendations and analytical reports
  • Conducting depository operations, deposits, and withdrawals
  • Providing technical support and customer service

Compliance with regulatory requirements:

  • Performing KYC (Know Your Client) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures
  • Providing reports to financial regulators
  • Identifying and preventing fraudulent activities
  • Fulfilling tax obligations and FATCA/CRS requirements

Improving the Qezvoro Invest platform:

  • Analyzing user behavior to improve UX/UI
  • Developing innovative products and functionality
  • Optimizing AI algorithms (using depersonalized data)
  • Conducting marketing research and trend analysis

Communication goals:

  • Notifications about the status of trading operations and changes in the account
  • Informing about new products, Qezvoro platform updates
  • Providing educational content and market analytics
  • Conducting service quality surveys

Legal Basis for Data Processing

+

Within the framework of the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the processing of your data is carried out on the following legal grounds:

  • Performance of contractual obligations: Processing necessary to fulfill the agreement on the provision of investment services
  • Legitimate interests of the company: Counteracting fraud, improving services, marketing communications with existing clients
  • Voluntary consent: Email newsletters, using analytical cookies, additional personalized services
  • Compliance with legal requirements: Performing AML/KYC procedures, tax reporting, compliance with regulatory standards

Principles of Data Transfer to Third Parties

+

Technology solution providers:

  • Cloud providers: Google Cloud Platform, AWS, Microsoft Azure for hosting and information processing
  • Payment systems: Acquiring banks, payment processors for conducting financial transactions
  • Information services: Bloomberg Terminal, Reuters for obtaining market data
  • KYC services: Onfido, Veriff for document verification and identification

Regulatory and supervisory authorities:

  • Financial supervisory authorities (SEC, FCA, BaFin, FINMA, etc.) within the framework of mandatory reporting
  • Tax authorities to fulfill FATCA, CRS, and national tax legislation requirements
  • Law enforcement agencies in the presence of justified official requests

Business partners:

  • Exchanges and brokerage companies for executing trade orders
  • Custodian banks for storing client assets
  • Insurance companies to provide insurance coverage

Important: We categorically DO NOT sell or lease your personal data to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Cross-border Data Transfer

+

Your personal data may be processed in jurisdictions outside the European Economic Area (EEA), including the USA, Switzerland, Singapore, and other countries where our technology partners are present.

Protection mechanisms for international transfers:

  • Decisions on adequacy by the European Commission for countries with a recognized adequate level of protection
  • Standard contractual clauses of the EU with partners in third countries
  • Binding Corporate Rules for intra-corporate data transfers
  • Special protection guarantees for transfers to the USA and other jurisdictions
