Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

General Privacy Policy+

Qezvoro Invest Ltd. ("we", "organization", "Qezvoro") pays maximum attention to the protection of your personal data and ensuring privacy. This Privacy Policy describes the methods of collecting, using, protecting and disclosing information about you when working with our services, including web resources, mobile applications and related tools (hereinafter - "Services").

Types of data collected+

Personal identification information

  • Basic information: full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact information: residential address, mobile phone, email
  • Financial data: information about income, sources of funds, investment experience
  • Verification documents: copies of passport, driver's license, bank statements

Information about activity on the platform

  • Transaction history: trading data, deposits, withdrawals, account balances
  • Technical details: IP addresses, browsers, operating systems, access timestamps
Purpose of personal data processing+

Provision of investment services:

  • Creating and managing user profiles
  • Execution of trading orders and investment operations
  • Generate personalized AI recommendations and analytics

Compliance with regulatory obligations:

  • Carrying out KYC (Know Your Client) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures
  • Filing reports with financial supervisory authorities
Principles of transfer of information to third parties+

We may transfer your data to the following categories of third parties:

  • Technology service providers: Cloud services (Google Cloud, AWS), payment systems, KYC services.
  • Regulatory and supervisory authorities: Financial regulators (SEC, FCA, CySEC).
  • Business partners: Exchanges and brokerage firms to execute trade orders.

Important: We absolutely DO NOT sell or rent your personal information to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Data protection and security measures+

Technical protection measures:

  • Cryptographic protection: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
  • Access control: multi-factor authentication, role-based permissions system.

Your rights (GDPR):

You have the right of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, and data portability. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@qezvoro.com.
