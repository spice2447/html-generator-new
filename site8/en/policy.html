Security and data protection measures+

Technical means of protection:

  • Cryptographic protection: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access control: multi-factor authentication, role-based authorization system
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 monitoring of threats and unauthorized access attempts
  • Reservation: regular backups in geographically distributed data centers

Organizational measures:

  • Employee training: systematic training on information security
  • Corporate Policies: strict internal regulations for the processing of personal data
  • Independent audits: annual security audits from EY and other audit firms
  • Data segregation: physical and logical separation of client and corporate information

Rights of personal data subjects

Under the GDPR, you are guaranteed the following rights:

  • Right of access: receiving a copy of your personal data and information about its processing
  • Right to rectification: correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"): deletion of personal data under certain conditions
  • Right to restriction of processing: suspension of processing of your data in specific cases
  • Right to data portability: obtaining data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to object: objection to data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to revoke consent: revoke consent to data processing at any time

Ways to exercise your rights:

  • Contacting support: privacy@qezvoro.com
  • Using the "Privacy Management" section in your personal account
  • Sending a written application to the company's postal address

Request review period: no more than 25 calendar days from the date of receipt.
