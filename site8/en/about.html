History of Qezvoro development - 2022 - Beginning of the revolution

2022 — Beginning of the revolution

2022 is the starting point of our development. It was then that the creation of Qezvoro as a comprehensive investment platform of international level began. It all started at the Austin Innovation Center, where a combined team of former Tesla and Two Sigma experts integrated machine intelligence and quantum finance expertise. The initial algorithm demonstrated 93% prediction accuracy, outperforming leading institutional advisors. Key achievements in 2022: Development of the basic AI architecture of the system, Launch of private alpha testing with a group of 1,200 participants from financial centers of America and Europe, In the first year of Qezvoro trading, the algorithms provided the test group with an average profitability of 71%, Raising $45M seed funding from Andreessen Horowitz, Obtaining initial licenses from CFTC, FCA and CySEC.
