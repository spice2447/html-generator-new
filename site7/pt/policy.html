Categories of information collected▾

Personal identification data

  • Basic information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, nationality
  • Contact details: Postal address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial profile: Information about income, sources of capital, trading experience and investment goals
  • Verification documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, income certificates, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photographic image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures

Qezvoro Platform Usage Data

  • Transaction history: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account status
  • Technical parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral analytics: Website navigation, session duration, user preferences, search queries

Information from External Sources

  • Partner details: Information from brokers, partner banks, market analysis providers
  • Open sources: Data from public records, media sources, social platforms (only publicly available information)
  • Consulting information: Information obtained through verification of recommendations and references

Regulatory compliance

  • Compliance with KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures

Improving the Qezvoro Invest Platform

Communication purposes
