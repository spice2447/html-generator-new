Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2025

Introduction to Personal Data Protection Policy▾

Qezvoro International Holdings Ltd. ("we", "company", "Qezvoro Invest") prioritizes the protection of your personal data and ensures confidentiality. This Privacy Policy outlines the principles of collecting, processing, protecting, and transferring information about you when using our services, including the web platform, mobile applications, and related tools (collectively - "Qezvoro platform").

By using the Qezvoro trading platform services, you consent to the processing of information in accordance with the principles set forth in this Privacy Policy.

Categories of Information Collected▾

Personal Identification Data

  • Basic Information: Full name, date of birth, place of birth, citizenship
  • Contact Details: Postal address, mobile phone number, email address
  • Financial Profile: Information on income, capital sources, trading experience, and investment goals
  • Verification Documents: Scans of passport, driver's license, income statements, bank statements
  • Biometrics: Photographic image for Know Your Customer (KYC) procedures

Qezvoro Platform Usage Data

  • Transaction History: Records of trading operations, deposits, withdrawals, account balances
  • Technical Parameters: IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, access timestamps
  • Behavioral Analytics: Website navigation, session duration, user preferences, search queries

Information from External Sources

  • Partner Data: Information from brokerage companies, partner banks, market analytics providers
  • Public Sources: Data from public registries, media sources, social platforms (exclusively publicly available information)
  • Referential Information: Information obtained from verification of recommendations and references
Purposes of Personal Data Processing▾

Provision of Investment Services:

  • Creation and administration of user accounts
  • Execution of trading orders and investment operations
  • Generation of personalized AI recommendations and analytical reports
  • Conducting depositary operations, deposits, and withdrawals
  • Provision of technical support and customer service

Compliance with Regulatory Requirements:

  • Execution of KYC (Know Your Customer) and AML (Anti-Money Laundering) procedures
  • Reporting to financial regulators
  • Identification and prevention of fraudulent activities
  • Fulfillment of tax obligations and FATCA/CRS requirements

Enhancement of Qezvoro Invest Platform:

  • Analysis of user behavior for UX/UI improvement
  • Development of innovative products and functionality
  • Optimization of AI algorithms (using depersonalized data)
  • Conducting marketing research and trend analysis

Communication Purposes:

  • Notifications about trading operation status and account changes
  • Information about new products, Qezvoro platform updates
  • Provision of educational content and market analytics
  • Conducting service quality surveys
Principles of Data Transfer to Third Parties▾

Technology Solution Providers:

  • Cloud Providers: Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform, Amazon Web Services for hosting and information processing
  • Payment Systems: Acquiring banks, payment processors for financial transactions
  • Information Services: Bloomberg Terminal, Refinitiv for market data access
  • KYC Services: Jumio, Sumsub for document verification and identification

Regulatory and Supervisory Authorities:

  • Financial supervisory bodies (SEC, FCA, ESMA, FINMA, etc.) for mandatory reporting
  • Tax authorities for FATCA, CRS, and national tax law compliance
  • Law enforcement agencies upon substantiated official requests

Business Partners:

  • Exchanges and brokerage firms for trade order execution
  • Depository banks for client asset safekeeping
  • Insurance organizations for insurance coverage

Important: We strictly DO NOT sell or rent your personal data to third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.

Data Security and Protection Measures▾

Technical Protection Measures:

  • Cryptographic Protection: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit
  • Access Management: Multi-factor authentication, role-based permissions system
  • Security Monitoring: 24/7 threat tracking and unauthorized access attempt monitoring
  • Backup: Regular backups in geographically distributed data centers

Organizational Measures:

  • Employee Training: Systematic information security training
  • Corporate Policies: Strict internal regulations for personal data processing
  • Independent Audits: Annual security checks by EY and other auditing firms
  • Data Segregation: Physical and logical separation of client and corporate information

Rights of Data Subjects:

In accordance with GDPR, you are guaranteed the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Obtaining a copy of your personal data and information about its processing
  • Right to Rectification: Correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Deletion of personal data under certain conditions
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: Suspension of your data processing in specific cases
  • Right to Data Portability: Receiving data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Protesting data processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdrawing consent for data processing at any time

Methods of Exercising Your Rights:

  • Contacting customer support: privacy@qezvoro.com
  • Using the "Privacy Management" section in your personal account
  • Sending a written request to the company's postal address

Request Processing Time: no more than 30 calendar days from the date of receipt.

Personal Data Retention Periods and Cookies▾

General Principles:

Personal data is stored only for the period necessary to achieve the processing purposes or in accordance with legislative requirements.

Specific Retention Periods:

  • Active Account Data: Entire contract period + 7 years after termination
  • KYC Documentation: 7 years after account closure (AML legislation requirements)
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (financial regulator requirements)
  • Marketing Data: Until consent withdrawal or 5 years from last activity
  • Phone Call Records: 5 years (MiFID II requirements)
  • Minors' Data: Immediate deletion upon detection

Use of Cookies and Similar Technologies:

Types of Cookies Used:

  • Essential Cookies: Ensure basic website functionality (login, security)
  • Functional Cookies: Save user preferences and settings
  • Analytical Cookies: Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics for resource usage analysis
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalize advertising content and track campaign effectiveness

Cookie Settings Management:

  • You can control cookie usage through:
  • Web browser settings
  • Cookie control panel on the Qezvoro website
  • Opting out of analytical cookies via Google Analytics Opt-out
